The night sky will host the “THE DREAM PLANET,” a unique and breathtaking celestial phenomenon, on June 3. Astronomers and stargazers alike are treated to a breathtaking visual display when multiple planets in our solar system align in a straight line during this occurrence. A rare chance to view multiple planets at once without a telescope exists when the planets align and look near to one another in the sky. The complex physics of our solar system can be appreciated in addition to being a visual feast.
